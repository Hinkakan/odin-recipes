Spaghetti Bolognese

Description

Spaghetti Bolognese. Just like in that movie.. you know.. the one with the dogs? and the italian guys singing.. what was the name?

Ingredients

  • 50 gr bacon
  • 2 onion
  • 2 garlic
  • 500 gr minced meat
  • 1 dl red wine
  • 2 cans of crushed tomatoes

Steps

  1. Fry the bacon. When done, put it on a plate with some paper on it
  2. Add onion and garlic to the pot with the bacon grease in it and fry until the onion is soft and transparent
  3. Add the meat and "brown" it
  4. Add red wine and crushed tomatoes and let it simmer for as long as possible
  5. Boil some salt water.
  6. Add spaghetti and let it boil for 15 minutes
