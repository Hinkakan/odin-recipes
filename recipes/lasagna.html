Lasagna

Description

A delicious lasagna that just melts in your mouth. The same type your dead, buried and now rotted grandmother used to make

Ingredients

  • 2 onions
  • 4 pieces of garlic
  • 400gr minced meat
  • 2 tblsp of oregano
  • 1 tblsp of timian
  • 4 carrots
  • 1 squash
  • 2 cans of tomato pur√©
  • 1 dl red wine
  • 1 pk Morney sauce
  • 125 gr mozzerella
  • 200gr lasagne pasta plates

Steps

  1. Sauter onions and garlic in olive oil until transparent
  2. Add minced meat and cook until fully "browned"
  3. Grate garrots and squash
  4. Add carrots, squash, oregano, timian, tomato and red wine to the pot and let it simmer for as long as possible
  5. Add the following 3 items in layers: Meatsauce, Morney sauce, pasta
  6. Finish by sprikling mozzerella on top
  7. Bake in oven for 35 minutes on 200 degrees
